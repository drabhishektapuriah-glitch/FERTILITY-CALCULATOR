Fertility Tips
Evidence-based guidance to maximise your chances of conception.
🏥 These tips are general guidance from Care and Cure Hospital, Bharuch. Always follow your doctor's specific advice for your case.
🎯
Time Intercourse Correctly
The fertile window is typically 5 days before ovulation + the day of ovulation. The 2 days before and on the day of ovulation are the most fertile (peak days).
📅
Frequency Matters
Aim for intercourse every 1–2 days during the fertile window. Daily intercourse during the fertile window gives the best chance of conception.
🌡️
Ovulation Signs
Watch for egg-white cervical mucus (clear, stretchy), mild one-sided pelvic pain (Mittelschmerz), and a slight rise in basal body temperature after ovulation.
🥗
Diet & Lifestyle
Take folic acid 5mg daily. Maintain a healthy BMI. Avoid smoking and alcohol. Reduce stress. A Mediterranean-style diet may support fertility.
💊
Supplements
Folic acid (pre-conception), Vitamin D, and CoQ10 (Coenzyme Q10) have evidence for fertility support. Consult your doctor before starting any supplements.
⏰
When to Seek Help
If you've been trying for 12 months (under 35) or 6 months (over 35) without conception, consult a fertility specialist. Earlier if you have PCOS, endometriosis, or irregular cycles.
📲
Track Your Cycles
Recording at least 3–6 months of LMP dates helps your doctor calculate your average cycle and identify the most accurate ovulation window.
